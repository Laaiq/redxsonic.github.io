The Idea

The Apartment Manager is a mobile system that enables landlords(Property owners) and their tenants communicate easily. The system will have a dashboard for apartment management, and there will be a mobile application for tenants to communicate with.

Why?

A lot of people living within the inner city of joburg are not from around the Joburg, They rely on apartments and rental flats for shelter. This process is feasible and effective. The maintanace side of things has proven over the years to be a hard task to execute. The apartment manager app aims at creating a funnel between the tenants and the landlords soo that complaints and notifications can be well structured and handled.
